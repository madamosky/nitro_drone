About Us

Nitro Drone Services, LLC operates in accordance of FAA regulations, providing aerial services that are safe and fully compliant with all aviation standards and regulations. On March 22nd, 2017, the FAA granted Nitro Drone Services the permission to operate unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) within the national airspace to conduct aerial imaging for real estate, construction, and inspections. We are FAA certified UAS pilots with FAA registered aircrafts and carry $1M liability insurance.
